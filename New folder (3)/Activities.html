Camel Ride, Shows, Dinner

First, enjoy a thrilling dune bashing ride with an experienced driver at the wheel, then climb aboard your own quad bike for an adrenaline-fueled adventure. Take part in other activities such as hookah or camel riding as you enjoy traditional Arab music and dancing at a Bedouin camp. A full buffet dinner is served in addition to tea, coffee and other refreshments.

Dinner/Lunch in the Sky Experience

Dinner in the Sky is where you get to dine while floating 50 meters above the ground! Located in Sky Dive Dubai, where a table for 22 people is suspended in the air with the help of a crane. This unique restaurant serves lunch, dinner, and afternoon tea as well. While you enjoy the 3-course meal served here, you get to check out the amazing attractions and landmarks of the city like the Palm Jumeirah, Burj Al Arab, Dubai Marina. All safety precautions are taken care of for the Dinner in the Sky experience.

Museum Of The Future in Dubai

The Museum of the Future is the first of its kind and aims to position visitors in an empowering version of the future. As the museum sits uniquely between skyscrapers, its ring shape represents the possibilities of mankind and humanity. Visit each of the immersive future environments found within the museum, each with their own distinctive themes. Through the merging aspects of science, technology, and spirituality, the museum aims to inspire anyone who visits.

Burj Khalifa: At the Top Observation Deck in Dubai

See an unforgettable view of Dubai from the observatory deck of the world’s tallest building, the Burj Khalifa. Head up to the tower’s 124th and 125th floor in just 35 seconds onboard the world's fastest elevator. Combine your visit to Burj Khalifa with various combo options like Cafe Treats, Dubai Aquarium, and many more! Marvel at the impressive bird’s-eye view of the city from the 152nd, 153rd and 154th floor.
